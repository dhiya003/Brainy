Privacy Policy

Effective: 13 July 2026

Brainy is a personal responsibility and reminder assistant. This policy explains the information used to provide its features.

Information you provide

Brainy may process responsibilities, dates, contact details, payment amounts, voice transcripts, images, screenshots and OCR text.

Local and native storage

Responsibilities are currently stored on your device. Android stores reminder data needed to schedule alarms and restore them after restart. Clearing app data or uninstalling may delete it.

AI processing

Text submitted for AI understanding is sent through a Supabase Edge Function to OpenAI. AI secret keys are not stored in the APK. Limited local capture remains available when AI is unavailable.

Voice, images and OCR

Voice input uses your device speech service. Image OCR is designed to run on-device with Google ML Kit. Brainy does not intentionally upload images selected only for OCR.

Notifications and external apps

Brainy uses alarm and notification permissions. Gmail, WhatsApp, dialler, Maps and UPI apps open only after user interaction. Brainy does not automatically send messages or complete payments.

Sharing and retention

Brainy does not sell personal information or use behavioural advertising. Local data remains until deleted, cleared or the app is removed. Cloud sync is not enabled in this test release.

Your choices

You may deny optional permissions, delete responsibilities, clear app data or uninstall Brainy.

Children and changes

Brainy is not directed to children under 13. This policy may be updated as features change. Questions may be raised through the Brainy GitHub repository.
